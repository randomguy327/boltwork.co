FAQ

Fair questions

Which tool do you use — n8n, Make.com or Zapier?

Whichever fits you. Zapier is fastest to start and easiest for your team to tweak. Make.com is the value pick for complex multi-step flows. n8n is self-hostable and cheapest at scale — best when you run many workflows or care about data staying on your own server. We're not affiliated with any of them, so we recommend what's right, not what pays us.

Will the AI say something wrong to my customers?

We design for this. Agents only act inside guardrails: they answer from your actual policies and order data, they escalate anything uncertain to a human, and during the first weeks everything runs in draft mode so you approve before anything is sent.

I'm not on Shopify — can you still help?

Yes. Shopify is our specialty, but n8n, Make and Zapier connect to WooCommerce, and most other platforms. If it has an API, we can automate it.

What happens if we stop working together?

You keep everything. Workflows are built in your accounts, documented, and handed over. No proprietary lock-in — if we're not delivering value, you should be able to walk away easily. That keeps us honest.

How fast can we go live?

A single automation is typically live within 1–2 weeks of the audit call. A full automation system takes 3–5 weeks, going live piece by piece so you see value early.
